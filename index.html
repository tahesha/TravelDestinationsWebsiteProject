Destination Description
Paris, France Known as the City of Love, Paris offers iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum.
Barcelona, Spain Famous for its unique architecture, vibrant culture, and beautiful beaches.
Tokyo, Japan The capital of Japan, is a bustling metropolis known for its modern skyscrapers, historic temples, and vibrant pop culture. From the serene gardens of the Imperial Palace to the neon-lit streets of Shibuya, Tokyo offers a blend of traditional and contemporary experiences.
Rio de Janeiro, Brazil Often called the "Cidade Maravilhosa" (Marvelous City), is famous for its stunning beaches, iconic landmarks like Christ the Redeemer statue, and vibrant carnival celebrations. With its breathtaking natural beauty and lively culture, Rio is a must-visit destination.
